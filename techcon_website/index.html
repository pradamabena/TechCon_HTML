The Africa Tech Festival 2024 is a premier event scheduled from November 12 to 14, 2024, at the Cape Town International Convention Centre in South Africa. This festival brings together over 15,000 attendees, 450 speakers, and 5,500 businesses from more than 100 countries. It offers a comprehensive view of how technological advancements are reshaping various sectors across Africa, including telecommunications, financial services, healthcare, and government. The festival explores innovations in AI, machine learning, 5G connectivity, data analytics, cybersecurity, and more, aiming to revolutionize African enterprises. Attendees can expect world-class speakers, industry-leading content, international exhibitions, and networking opportunities focused on delivering real-world impact through technological integration.
